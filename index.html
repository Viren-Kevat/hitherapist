Meet Your Therapist

Trauma-informed, integrative, and dedicated to your healing journey

Licensed Marriage & Family Therapist Ayurvedic Health Counselor 12+ Years Experience

Johanna Estevez, LMFT, AHC

Johanna Estevez is a trauma-informed, Afro-Dominican licensed clinician. As a trained integrative therapist, Johanna believes in treating the whole person by addressing mental, physical and spiritual imbalances. She combines traditional healing practices with modern techniques, customizing each session to meet the unique needs of clients.

Johanna has 12 years of experience working with clients on reducing stress, finding clarity and breaking unhealthy patterns. Her compassionate nature allows her to create a safe and nurturing space for clients to explore their innermost thoughts and feelings. Johanna is committed to empowering clients to tap into their innate healing abilities and embrace a lifestyle of wellness and vitality.

Achieve Your Wellness Goals with HITS

At Holistic Integrative Therapy Services, we believe in the power of holistic wellness to transform lives.

Our approach is rooted in the philosophy that every individual is unique and requires personalized care; we focus on creating a safe and empathetic space for individuals on their journey towards healing and self-discovery.

Our commitment to integrity ensures that every therapeutic interaction is conducted with the utmost ethical standards and transparency. Through this mission, we aim to empower individuals to navigate life's challenges with resilience, authenticity, and a profound sense of well-being.
